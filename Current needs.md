## Customer Contact List

Purpose:
Show me only the people who should be contacted for the current event.

Rules:
- Exclude anyone who has already purchased tickets.
- Exclude anyone marked "Do Not Text."
- Include people who attended one of the last two dances.
- Allow searching by name or phone number.
- Show their usual number of tickets.
- Show the last event they attended.
